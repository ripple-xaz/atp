Atlas Protocol (ATP) is a decentralized digital marketing protocol built on blockchain. ATP re-establishes relationships between multiple parties, e.g.advertisers, publishers and users.

ATP will be the first application level protocol to be built on Nebulas Blockchain, allowing all participants involved to benefit from decentralized digital marketing.

Nebulas is a next generation public blockchain, capable of providing Decentralized Search Engine Framework for data on blockchain. ATP will utilize Nebulas technology of Nebulas Rank (NR) and Developer Incentive Protocol (DIP).
